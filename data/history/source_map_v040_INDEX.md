## The Wandjina (treated with care)

This tradition is alive, sacred, and held by specific custodial communities — the Worrorra, Ngarinyin, and Wunambal peoples of the Kimberley. The database represents it descriptively and respectfully, pointing toward the tradition rather than claiming to contain it.

**Cosmology (Mowanjum tradition):**
- **Idjair** — first Wandjina, lives in the Milky Way, father of all Wandjinas. Withdrawn.
- **Wallungunder** — Idjair's son, the active creator. Created the earth and all life on it. Created the first humans, the **Gyorn Gyorn** (Gwion Gwion) people. Returned to Idjair to bring back more Wandjinas to give the Gyorn Gyorn the **Law**.
- Three primary Wandjinas, one per Mowanjum people: **Namarali** (Worrorra), **Wodjin/Wanalirri** (Ngarinyin), **Rimijmarra/Gulingi** (Wunambal).
- **Wunggurr** — the Kimberley Rainbow Serpent creator-being, a related but distinct figure.

**The mouthless face is theologically loaded.** The Wandjina has no mouth because verbal speech is unnecessary for a being whose power acts directly by will. The body covered in dots represents falling rain. Eyes represent thunderstorms. The line between the eyes is a power-line for transferring energy.

**Parallels drawn with appropriate caution:**
- Idjair as withdrawn-Milky-Way-father → partial-parallel to Canaanite El, Hebrew El Elyon, PIE *Dyḗus Ph₂tḗr
- Wallungunder as active-creator-son → partial-parallel to Baal-as-active-under-El
- Namarali as rain-fertility-storm-god → partial-parallel to Baal Hadad, Indra, Perkʷunos, Thor
- Wunggurr as primordial-water creator-being → partial-parallel to Vedic Dānu

Every Wandjina parallel is flagged with a scholarly_caveat noting that the Worrorra/Ngarinyin/Wunambal frameworks have their own integrity and should not be reduced to instances of imported categories.

- **Talmud Niddah 61a**: Og of Bashan is the grandson of Shemhazai the fallen Watcher (Shemhazai → Ahijah → Og). The single most important rabbinic text for threading the pre-flood Watcher tradition to the post-flood Rephaim line.

The convergence: **All these traditions point to the same religious-geographic complex centered on Bashan / Golan / Mt Hermon / Hebron in the Levant.** Three independent sources (Ugaritic, biblical, Enochic) place giant / divine-rebel cult in the same mountains. The Egyptian execration texts attest the southern (Hebron) anchor independently.

## The Global Giants Complex — 17+ traditions

The framework `global-giants-complex` catalogs giants across:

1. Hebrew Bible (Nephilim, Rephaim, Anakim, etc.)
2. Second Temple Apocryphal (Watchers, Book of Giants)
3. Egyptian (Iy-ʿanaq)
4. Ugaritic / Canaanite (rpum)
5. Mesopotamian (Apkallu, Humbaba, Anzu)
6. Greek (Titans, Gigantes, Cyclopes)
7. Norse (Jötnar — Frost Giants, Mountain Giants)
8. Celtic Irish (Fomorians)
9. Vedic / Puranic (Daityas, Danavas, Rakshasas)
10. Aztec / Nahua (Quinametzin)
11. Inca / Andean (first inhabitants)
12. Paiute / Great Basin (Si-Te-Cah)
13. Other Native American (Stone Coats, Nahullo, Maushop, Waziya)
14. Chinese (Pangu, Kua Fu, Xing Tian)
15. Japanese (Daidarabotchi)
16. African (Yoruba creator-giants)
17. Proto-Indo-European reconstructed (*Yemo)

Each entry includes primary terms, named individuals (where attested), and key textual sources.

### Structural features that recur across them
- Elder, larger race predating present humanity
- Divine or semi-divine parentage (often via interbreeding)
- Builders of megaliths and civilization
- Teachers of forbidden arts (Levantine-cluster specific)
- Sins or pride leading to destruction
- Destruction by flood / fire / cosmic cataclysm / war
- Bones / remains attested in the landscape
- Toponymic preservation (Bashan, Cholula, Lovelock, etc.)
- Imprisonment of a key figure awaiting final judgment

### Why this matters
Even if all instances are entirely independent in origin, the convergence on a single structural pattern across cultures separated by oceans and millennia indicates that the human imagination is constrained to a small set of possible stories about its own deep past. That convergence is the spinal column of the *Parallels of the Gods* project.
